Alice Rose Web Developer

Based in Berlin, Germany, I have been building beautiful, performant digital products at A Color Bright since 2015.

Prior to that, I spent about 8 years working as a professional software developer on a range of products in New Zealand, Australia and Germany.

I particularly enjoy the problem-solving aspects of web development. Designing app architecture, building required functionality, improving performance, ensuring accessibility, and providing an excellent user experience are all extremely satisfying and rewarding. I do my best work as part of a strong team.

I also enjoy teaching and coach regularly at Django Girls events in Germany, in both English and German.

I speak German fluently and have a strong interest in linguistics and language-learning. I can usually be found dabbling in one language or another.
